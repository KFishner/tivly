Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for tivly?

tivly is currently in a private beta, signup for our newsletter and we will let you know when a spot opens up!

What kinds of rewards can I get through tivly?

All kinds! The loyalty rewards are completely customized by the restaurants, so tell your favorite places what rewards you want to see!

Do I have to enter my credit card to participate?

Nope! You can take recommendations from friends and give recommendations without registering your card. However, to grow your loyalty points and redeem for rewards, you must register your card.

How do you keep my information secure?

We take every step to protect your information. We don't store your credit card information on our servers, we use a service called CardSpring that protects your information with PCI compliant bank-level security.

What is the process of registering my business with tivly?

We leverage a unique technology that allows us to eliminate all hardware and software installation. Give us your merchant ID, and we will have tivly up and running for your business in one week! Works with all payment cards and processors.
